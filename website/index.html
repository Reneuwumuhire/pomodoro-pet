EVERYTHING, NOTHING IN THE WAY

Small window. Serious focus tools.

Sound that sets the mood

Built-in lo-fi and ambient loops — rain, ocean, white noise, café — each with its own volume. Point it at any folder of your own songs and it plays them as a playlist; fast-forward or skip right from the speaker grille, which dances to whatever’s playing.

Strict mode that bites

List the sites that derail you. During a focus session they get yanked to a blank page the moment you open them — and a full-screen breather takes over your breaks.

Tasks & pomodoros

Add what you’re working on, estimate it in 🍅, and watch the count fill as you go.

Stats & streaks

Daily and weekly focus, time by tag, and a streak that’s genuinely hard to break.

Lives in the menu bar

Drops down from the tray as a draggable little device you can park anywhere — or pop out a tiny always-on-top widget (⌘⇧M) when you want it closer.
